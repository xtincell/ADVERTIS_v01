## 📋 DOCUMENT OVERVIEW

### Purpose
Document E is the operational heart of the strategy. It translates brand positioning into concrete campaigns, actions, and activations that engage target audiences and build superfans.

### Audience
- CMO / Marketing Directors
- Campaign Managers
- Creative Teams
- Agency Partners
- Execution Teams

### Key Deliverables
- **3-6 Detailed Campaigns** (full specification each)
- **Customer Journey Maps**
- **Touchpoint Strategy**
- **Content Strategy**
- **Activation Playbook**
- **Community Building Plan**

## 🎯 SECTION 4: CAMPAIGN PORTFOLIO (Detailed Spec)

### Campaign Template Structure

Each campaign MUST include ALL these fields:

#### 4.X.1 Campaign Overview
```yaml
campaign_name: "[Campaign Name]" *
campaign_tagline: "[Short tagline if applicable]"
campaign_status: "[Planned/In Progress/Completed]" *
campaign_period: "[Start Date] - [End Date]" *
campaign_budget_total: "{montant} {devise}" *  # Ex: "270M FCFA" ou "€450 000" selon marché cible
campaign_type: "[Launch/Seasonal/Always-On/Community/Content/etc.]" *
```

#### 4.X.2 Objectif (Strategic Objectives)
**Format:** Numbered list of 4-6 SMART objectives

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
objectives:
  - objective_1: *
      description: "[What we want to achieve]"
      metric: "[How we measure it]"
      target: "[Specific target number/percentage]"
      timeframe: "[When we achieve it]"
  - objective_2: *
  - objective_3: *
  - objective_4:
  - objective_5:
  - objective_6:
```

**Example:**
```
Objectif 1: Generate 25% brand awareness in Lagos, Nairobi, Accra
  - Metric: Unaided brand awareness surveys
  - Target: 25% awareness (from 8% baseline)
  - Timeframe: Within 3 months of launch

Objectif 2: Acquire 500 superfans in first 3 months
  - Metric: Superfan definition criteria met
  - Target: 500 superfans
  - Timeframe: Q1 2026
```

#### 4.X.3 Big Idea
**Format:** Headline + 2-3 paragraph narrative

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
big_idea_headline: "[Compelling headline that encapsulates the idea]" *
big_idea_narrative: "[2-3 paragraphs explaining the idea, why it matters, how it connects emotionally]" *
big_idea_essence: "[One-sentence distillation]" *
```

**Example:**
```
Headline: "The Revolution Starts With What You Wear"

Narrative: We're not just selling clothes—we're igniting a movement. Every AMARA
piece is an act of cultural reclamation, a statement of pride, and a vote for
sustainable fashion. When you wear AMARA, you're not following trends; you're
creating a revolution.

Essence: Fashion as cultural activism and self-expression.
```

#### 4.X.4 Axe Créatif (Creative Direction)
**Format:** Structured breakdown of creative approach

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
creative_direction:
  visual: *
    description: "[Visual style, aesthetic, mood]"
    references: "[Inspiration, mood boards]"

  tone: *
    description: "[Tone of voice, personality]"
    examples: "[Example copy/messaging]"

  message: *
    core_message: "[What we're saying]"
    supporting_messages: "[Secondary messages]"

  execution_principles: *
    - "[Principle 1]"
    - "[Principle 2]"
    - "[Principle 3]"
```

**Example:**
```
VISUAL:
Bold, vibrant colors inspired by African textiles. Strong geometric patterns.
Natural light photography showcasing real people in real environments. Authentic,
not staged.

TONE:
Confident, empowering, unapologetic. We celebrate African identity without being
preachy. Inspiring without being aspirational. Direct but warm.

MESSAGE:
Core: "Your heritage is your superpower. Wear it boldly."
Supporting: Authenticity > trends, Quality > quantity, Community > consumerism

EXECUTION PRINCIPLES:
1. Show real artisans, real process, real impact
2. No models - real customers, real stories
3. Bold typography, generous white space
4. Always tie back to heritage/craft/sustainability
```

#### 4.X.5 Pistes Créatives (Creative Directions)
**Format:** 3-4 distinct creative concepts/approaches

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
creative_directions:
  - direction_1: *
      name: "[Direction Name]"
      concept: "[Brief description]"
      execution: "[How it would be executed]"
      strengths: "[Why this works]"

  - direction_2: *
  - direction_3: *
  - direction_4:
```

**Example:**
```
PISTE 1: Artisan Portraits
Concept: Hero content featuring artisans crafting pieces, telling their stories,
showing technique and tradition.
Execution: Documentary-style video series (60-90 sec each), photo essays,
Instagram Stories takeovers by artisans.
Strengths: Authentic, educational, builds emotional connection, differentiates
from competitors.

PISTE 2: Urban Heritage
Concept: Street fashion meets tradition—modern Africans rocking AMARA in
contemporary urban settings.
Execution: Lookbook shoots in Lagos/Nairobi/Accra streets, user-generated
content campaign, influencer partnerships.
Strengths: Aspirational yet accessible, shows versatility, targets primary
audience directly.

PISTE 3: Pattern Stories
Concept: Close-ups of patterns with captions explaining cultural significance
and meaning.
Execution: Social media carousel posts, product detail pages, hang tags on
garments, exhibition.
Strengths: Educational, story-driven, works across all channels, evergreen content.
```

#### 4.X.7 Secteur / Target
**Format:** Detailed target audience specification

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
target_audience:
  primary: *
    segment_name: "[Name of primary segment]"
    demographics: "[Age, location, income, etc.]"
    psychographics: "[Values, interests, lifestyle]"
    behaviors: "[Shopping, media consumption, etc.]"
    pain_points: "[What problems do they have?]"
    motivations: "[What drives their purchases?]"

  secondary:
    [Same structure as primary]

  geographic_focus: *
    - "[City/Region 1]"
    - "[City/Region 2]"
```

**Exemple :**
```
PRIMARY: Millennials Urbains Africains
  - Demographics: 25-35 ans, professionnels urbains, revenu ménage
    18M-48M FCFA/an (~€30k-€80k)
  - Psychographics: Value authenticity, culturally proud, environmentally
    conscious, socially aware, digitally native
  - Behaviors: Shop online and in-store, follow fashion influencers, research
    purchases, willing to pay premium for quality
  - Pain Points: Disconnect from heritage, guilt about fast fashion, lack of
    authentic African fashion options
  - Motivations: Self-expression, cultural connection, supporting artisans,
    sustainability, quality over quantity

SECONDARY: Gen Z Early Adopters
  - Demographics: 18-24, students and young creatives, lower disposable income
  - Role: Trend-setters, influencers, brand ambassadors
  - Focus: Social media engagement, word-of-mouth, aspirational purchases

GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS:
  - Lagos, Nigeria (primary market)
  - Nairobi, Kenya (secondary market)
  - Accra, Ghana (tertiary market)
```

#### 4.X.13 Success Metrics

**Required Fields:**
```yaml
success_metrics:
  primary_metrics: *
    - metric_1:
        name: "[Metric name]"
        target: "[Specific target]"
        measurement: "[How measured]"

  secondary_metrics:
    - metric_1:
        [Same structure]

  roi_target: *
    investment: "{montant} {devise}"  # Ex: "45M FCFA" ou "€450 000"
    expected_return: "{montant} {devise}"
    roi_multiple: X.Xx
    break_even_date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
```

**Example:**
```
PRIMARY METRICS:

1. Brand Awareness
   Target: 25% unaided brand awareness
   Measurement: Monthly brand tracking surveys in Lagos/Nairobi/Accra

   Target: 500 superfans
   Measurement: Customers meeting superfan criteria (2+ purchases, 1+ referral,
                community engagement)
   Baseline: 0
   Timeline: Achieve by end of Q1 2026

3. Revenue Generation
   Target: 28M FCFA (~€46k) [marché local] / €280 000 [diaspora/international]
   Measurement: Ventes e-commerce + ventes événements directement attribuées à la campagne
   Baseline: 0 (nouvelle marque)
   Timeline: Cumulative through Q1 2026

SECONDARY METRICS:

4. Social Engagement
   Target: 20,000 combined followers, 8%+ engagement rate
   Measurement: Instagram + TikTok + Twitter analytics

5. Press Coverage
   Target: 15+ quality media placements
   Measurement: PR tracking, 10M+ earned media impressions

6. Event Participation
   Target: 500+ total event attendees across 3 cities
   Measurement: RSVP + attendance tracking

7. Influencer Performance
```
